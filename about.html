About BizBoost Kenya

Empowering Kenyan businesses with digital innovation and creative marketing strategies.

Our Story

BizBoost Kenya was founded with a simple yet powerful goal: to bridge the digital divide for small and medium-sized businesses in Kenya. In an era where online presence is everything, many local businesses were being left behind.

We started as a small team in Nairobi, driven by the passion to see local brands compete on a global scale. Today, we are proud to be the catalyst for growth for dozens of businesses across the country, turning clicks into loyal customers.
